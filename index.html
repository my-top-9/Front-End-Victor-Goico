My Top Nine

Do you remember MySpace Top 8 friends? Well this is MyTopNine, a social networking site that allows users to select their TopNine in a variety of categories.

Why MyTopNine?

Compatibility

MyTopNine allows you to meet new and interesting people. You can learn a lot about what kind of person somebody is just by checking their categories! Connect with people who have common interests, hobbies, or even careers!

How It Works

Click on any profile, and you'll immediately be met by a list of nine categories, and the profile's top choice for each category. Sports, videogames, books, movies, you name it! Finding people who love the same things (or maybe don't if you're trying to mix it up) has never been easier!
